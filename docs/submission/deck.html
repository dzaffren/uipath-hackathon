The gap

Problem & solution

Problem
Thin rationale. The recorded close/escalate reason is one line — it can't be defended when a regulator reviews it a year later.
Scattered evidence. KYC, transactions, sanctions/PEP and adverse media sit in separate systems; assembling them eats the time meant for judgment.
Inconsistent calls. Outcomes vary by analyst, and the high-volume low-risk bucket is a governance blind spot.
Solution
Risk-tier BPMN flow. Maestro routes every alert to proportional human review.
Validated citations. The agent cites only real evidence IDs — invented citations are flagged before a human sees them.
Maker–checker on high-risk. A challenger disputes the call · sign/override on medium · batch sign-off + 5% QA on low.
Conservative by construction. Red-flag overrides + a confidence floor, and an append-only audit record.
